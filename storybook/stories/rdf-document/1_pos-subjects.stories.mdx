## pos-subjects

Shows a list of all things that occur as a subject in an RDF document resource. The
surrounding `<pos-resource>` should therefore refer to an RDF document.

  <Story name="pos-subjects">
    {({ uri }) => html`
      <pos-resource uri=${uri}>
        <pos-subjects />
      </pos-resource>
    `}
  </Story>
