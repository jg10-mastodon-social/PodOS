## pos-make-findable

A button, to make a thing discoverable through search, by adding it to a label index.

For this to work, you need to be signed in and have a [private label index](https://github.com/pod-os/PodOS/blob/main/docs/features/full-text-search.md).

> ATTENTION: To be able to log in from here, you have to go to the `Canvas` tab and click
> the `Open canvas in new tab` button in the upper right corner of the page
